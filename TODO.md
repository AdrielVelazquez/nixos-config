- `local.apple-studio-display-hbr3` system-manager service in `modules/system-manager/apple-studio-display-hbr3.nix`: target `systemConfigs.cachyos-framework`. Framework 13 / CachyOS with Apple Studio Display repeatedly hotplugs at native 5120x2880@60. Debugfs showed the default link sometimes using HBR2 (`Current: 4 0x14 0`) even though the connector reported and verified HBR3 (`4 0x1e 16`). Writing HBR3 changed the active link to `Current: 4 0x1e 0`, matching the CachyOS forum workaround for avoiding the HBR2+DSC path (https://discuss.cachyos.org/t/fix-apple-studio-display-5k-flicker-black-screen-on-linux-with-amd-macbookpro16-1-by-forcing-displayport-hbr3-and-avoiding-dsc/29495), but later flickers still occurred while already at HBR3. On 2026-06-04, full USB4 resets renumbered the Studio Display connector from DP-7/DP-8 to DP-5/DP-6, so the service was changed to scan all DP-* debugfs link settings by default instead of hardcoding DP-7. This service is therefore a Framework-only stabilizer to keep the link from falling back to HBR2, not a proven full fix for the remaining HPD or USB4 drops. Related Framework report: https://community.frame.work/t/apple-studio-display-flickers-after-firmware-update-to-0-0-3-5/82587. Upstream/default direction: remove this service once the AMD/USB4/Studio Display path no longer falls back to HBR2 or once a kernel/firmware fix makes the debugfs preference unnecessary; validate by confirming the active Studio Display DP connector remains at HBR3 after reboot/hotplug without the service and by running `nix eval .#systemConfigs.cachyos-framework.drvPath --show-trace`.
